# Intern Projects

## Open Projects

- [Intel XPU GEMM Scheduling Optimization](01-intel-xpu-gemm-optimization.md): map
  Hopper-style Hilbert curve scheduling and the CA strategy from recent GEMM
  scheduling work to Intel XPU.
- [SGLang MoE Expert Prefetching with Weight Offload](02-sglang-moe-expert-prefetch-offload.md):
  combine SGLang weight offload with MoE expert-route prediction to serve larger
  models on small-memory Intel XPU cards.
- [Enable Qwen3-TTS on SGLang Omni for Intel XPU](03-sglang-omni-qwen3-tts-xpu.md):
  bring up Qwen3-TTS on SGLang Omni, profile GPU utilization and kernel
  hotspots, then optimize the measured bottleneck on Intel XPU.
